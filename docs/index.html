About US

The Underground Coffee Company is a student-run, not-for-profit coffee shop at Brown University. We are located in the lower level of the Stephen Robert '62 Campus Center. 

The Underground is run by a team of passionate and devoted students who aim to provide the Brown and Providence community with a cozy and dynamic space for conversation, experimentation, and creation. Commited to offering the best caffeine fix and sweet treats on Brown's campus, we serve George Howell Coffee, roasted in Boston, and Knead Doughnuts, made fresh daily in downtown Providence.

 Welcome! Make yourself at home.
